Abstract

We propose a program in which light (and the invariant speed c) is the primitive from which spacetime, fields, and matter emerge. In this view, space is a relational structure induced by light-like connectivity; mass corresponds to confined or resonant light; and gravity arises as geometry that constrains null propagation. We outline axioms, a minimal formalism, falsifiable predictions, and simulation plans.

The approach aims to (i) recover special and general relativity from null-cone structure, (ii) reproduce the Standard Model particle spectrum as stable resonances of constrained radiation, and (iii) explain ‚Äúdark‚Äù phenomena via non-radiative light dynamics rather than new matter species.

    Axioms

    1. Light primacy. The invariant causal speed c defines structure; ‚Äúspace‚Äù and ‚Äútime‚Äù are emergent summaries of light-like relations.
